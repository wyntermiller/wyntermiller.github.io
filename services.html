Editorial Services

Editorial services are priced on a sliding scale, based on the scope of the project, the state of the draft, and the timeline for editorial work. Below is a sample of packages and prices. Interested in something not listed? Email me! I'm happy to chat through options and design a package that fits your project.

Short Story Consultation

Designed for short stories only (not novel excerpts)

  • Includes line edits, developmental editing with in-line notes, and a 30-minute consultation call
  • Does not include an editorial letter
  • Prices start at $250 for 6K words

Line Edit

Designed for writers who need to polish at the line level

  • Includes a review of spelling, grammar, word choice, and sentence structure, with a focus on enhancing clarity and readability
  • Does not include in-line notes, an editorial letter, or a consultation call
  • Prices start at $1,000 for 50K words

Standard Consultation

Designed for writers who have a complete manuscript and need a holistic review

  • Includes a close reading of the manuscript, and a one-hour phone or video call structured as an editorial conversation with a focus on structure, thematic development, and characterization
  • Does not include line edits, in-line notes, or an editorial letter
  • Prices start at $1,500 for 80K words

Developmental Edit

Designed for writers at any stage of the drafting process who need a broad but deep look at chronology, structure, and voice, as well as thematic, character, and plot development

  • Includes in-line notes, a detailed editorial letter, and a one-hour phone or video consultation call
  • Does not include line edits
  • Prices start at $2,500 for 80K words

Comprehensive Consultation

Designed for writers at later stages of the drafting process

  • Includes everything: line edits, developmental editing with in-line notes, an editorial letter, and a one-hour consultation call
  • Prices start at $3,000 for 80K words
